\# Frequently Asked Questions

\#\# Do you offer same-day delivery?  
Yes, for orders placed before 2:00 PM Monday–Saturday.

\#\# Can I request a specific delivery time?  
We cannot guarantee exact delivery times.

\#\# What if the recipient is not home?  
We will leave the arrangement in a safe place if possible.

\#\# Are arrangements pet-safe?  
Some flowers can be toxic to pets. Please consult us before ordering if pets are present.
