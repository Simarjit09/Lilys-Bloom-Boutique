\# Flower Care Instructions

\#\# General Care  
\- Trim stems at a 45-degree angle before placing in water.  
\- Change water every 2 days.  
\- Keep away from direct sunlight and heat.  
\- Remove wilted blooms promptly.

\#\# Roses  
\- Remove guard petals before display.  
\- Re-trim stems after 2 days.

\#\# Lilies  
\- Remove pollen to prevent staining.
